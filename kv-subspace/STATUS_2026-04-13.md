- vLLM uses custom TRITON kernels (`vllm::unified_kv_cache_update`, `vllm::unified_attention`)
- Compression must happen at kernel level (Python hooks too slow)
- No clean injection point without forking vLLM codebase
- Timeline: Would need 2-3 days to implement properly
